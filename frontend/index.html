Our Philosophy

Indian classical music is a continuous pitch system, not equal-tempered notes. SvarAI doesn't "snap" to notes or give right/wrong scores. Instead, we show you how you're singing—your pitch trajectories, oscillation patterns, and settling behaviors—so you can develop authentic expression in your chosen tradition.
